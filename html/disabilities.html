White Cane Law and Disabilities

Overview

This set of posters are intended to spread awareness about disability laws and how they're tied into public transportation in Cambridge Massachusetts. Most of it is based on the White Cane Law and how it is put into practice. We chose this because it's a topic most people have general knowledge on but aren't aware of the details.
