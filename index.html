Cambridge Caution

Cambridge Caution consists of projects to promote traffic safety and awareness throughout the city of Cambridge. Our projects focus on critical issues such as Right of Way, Signal Usage, Distracted Driving, White Cane Law and disabilities, and Bike safety and rules. Our goal is to ensure safer streets for everyone, from pedestrians to cyclists and motorists, by educating the community on responsible and mindful behaviors through the streets of Cambridge.
